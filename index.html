Simple Summary: Garbage Tracker does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data with anyone. Everything stays on your device, period.

What Information We Collect

None. Garbage Tracker does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics. The app works entirely offline without any internet connection required.

How Your Data is Stored

  • Pickup Schedules: Stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
  • Custom Waste Types: Stored locally on your device only
  • Notification Settings: Stored locally on your device only
  • App Preferences: Stored locally on your device only

Important: If you delete the app, all your data is permanently deleted with it. We cannot recover it because we never had access to it.

Notifications

The app requests permission to send local notifications to remind you about garbage pickups. These notifications are:

  • Generated locally on your device
  • Do not transmit any data to external servers
  • Can be disabled at any time in iOS Settings

Data Sharing

We don't share anything because we don't collect anything. Your garbage pickup schedule is completely private and stays only on your device.

Third-Party Services

Garbage Tracker does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is transmitted to any external services.

Children's Privacy

Our app is completely safe for users of all ages as it does not collect any personal information whatsoever.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device using iOS secure storage, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features including device passcode, Touch ID, or Face ID.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing for us to delete, modify, or export. You have complete control over your data directly through the app.
